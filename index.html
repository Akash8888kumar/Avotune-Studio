Journey Map

Course Structure

A clearly mapped 6-month journey from complete beginner to professional music producer.

Month 1–2
Foundations & Sound Design

Music theory basics, DAW setup, synth programming, drum design, and creating your first complete beat from scratch.

Month 3
Arrangement & Composition

Song structure, arrangement techniques, building tension and release, genre-specific workflows, and working with samples.

Month 4
Mixing Deep Dive

Advanced EQ, multi-band compression, bus processing, parallel chains, and reference mixing on studio monitors.

Month 5
Mastering & Delivery

Stereo mastering, stem mastering, LUFS targets for DSPs, vinyl prep, and building your official EP portfolio.

Month 6
Business, Brand & Launch

Distribution strategy, social media for artists, pitching to labels, sync licensing, and your graduation showcase.

Contact Us
Do I need prior music knowledge to join?
Absolutely not! The MMPM course is designed for complete beginners. We start with the fundamentals of music theory and build up gradually. If you already have some experience, our instructor will ensure you're challenged at the right level.
What equipment do I need to bring?
Nothing! All studio equipment, DAW licences, plugins, and hardware are provided at Avotune Studio. For home practice, we recommend a laptop with at least 8GB RAM — we'll guide you on free DAW options to start with.
Is the course online or offline?
The core studio sessions are conducted offline at our studio in Delhi. We also provide a supplementary online learning portal with recorded lessons so you can revise anytime. A hybrid format is available for outstation students.
What is the fee structure?
The full program fee is ₹45,000 for 6 months. We offer EMI options, a ₹2,500 early bird discount, and a group discount for friends enrolling together. Fill the interest form and we'll share the complete fee breakup.
Will I get a job after the course?
We provide active placement support — connecting students with recording studios, production houses, and label opportunities in our network. Over 80% of our graduates are actively working in the music industry within 3 months of completion.
