Got FAQs?

Contact Us
Do I need prior music knowledge?
Not at all! MMPM is designed for complete beginners. We start from music theory fundamentals and build up gradually. If you have experience, our instructor will ensure you're challenged at the right level.
What equipment do I need to bring?
Nothing! All studio equipment, DAW licences, plugins, and hardware are provided at Avotune Studio. For home practice, a laptop with 8GB+ RAM is recommended — we'll guide you on free starter options.
Is the course online or offline?
Core sessions are conducted offline at our Delhi studio. We also provide an online learning portal with recorded lessons for revision. A hybrid format is available for outstation students.
What is the fee structure?
The full program is ₹45,000 for 6 months. We offer EMI options, a ₹2,500 early bird discount, and group discounts for friends enrolling together. Fill the interest form and we'll share the complete fee breakup.
Will I get a job after the course?
We provide active placement support — connecting students with recording studios, production houses, and labels in our network. Over 80% of our graduates are actively working in the music industry within 3 months.
Can I switch batches or pause the course?
Yes! Life happens. We allow batch switches with at least 2 weeks' notice. You can also pause for up to 4 weeks in genuine circumstances. All recorded content remains accessible throughout your enrollment period.
