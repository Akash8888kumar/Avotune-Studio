Our Story

Built By Artists,
For Artists

We didn't build Avotune to be another music school. We built it because we believed every talented producer in India deserves access to world-class training — not just those who can afford to study abroad.

Talent is equally distributed. Opportunity is not. We exist to fix that — one producer at a time.

At Avotune, we measure success not by how many students enroll — but by how many are working professionally in music within a year of graduating. That north star shapes every decision we make: our curriculum, our gear investments, our batch sizes, and how we hire instructors.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Everything we do — from curriculum design to how we talk to students — flows from these five principles.

01

Passion First

We only teach what we love. Every instructor at Avotune is an active working professional — not someone who used to make music and now just talks about it.

02

Hands-On Always

We don't believe in passive learning. From day one, students are making music on professional gear in a real acoustic environment.

03

Small Batches

We cap every batch at 20 students. Not because we can't handle more — because individual attention is non-negotiable for real skill development.

04

Outcomes Over Certificates

We're not a certificate mill. We track graduates and obsess over how many are actively working in music. That's our real report card.

05

Open Access

World-class music training shouldn't require a trip to London or Los Angeles. We're bringing it to New Delhi — and soon, online to every city in India.

The Journey

How We Got Here

2015
The Bedroom Studio

Aryan starts producing music from a tiny bedroom in Delhi with a ₹15,000 laptop and a borrowed MIDI keyboard. His first track gets 800 plays.

2017
First Bollywood Credit

A sync placement on a Bollywood indie film opens doors. Aryan moves into a proper studio space and begins taking on production clients.

2019
The First Student

A friend asks Aryan to teach him production. Word spreads. By year-end, 12 students are paying for informal one-on-one sessions. The idea for a proper school forms.

2020
Avotune Studio Founded

The first official batch of MMPM launches with 18 students in a rented Lajpat Nagar studio. 16 out of 18 go on to work professionally in music within 12 months.

2022
New Studio, New Gear

We move into our current 3,000 sq ft space, add a recording booth, Neve console, and a dedicated mastering suite. The curriculum doubles in depth.

2025
500+ Alumni & Growing

500+ graduates are now working across Bollywood, independent music, streaming, gaming audio, and content creation. Batch 12 is about to begin.
