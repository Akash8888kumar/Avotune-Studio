Quick Answers

Common Questions

How quickly do you respond to enquiries?
We respond to all messages within 24 hours on weekdays. For urgent matters, WhatsApp is the fastest way to reach us — we typically reply within the hour during studio hours.
Can I visit the studio before enrolling?
Absolutely! We encourage prospective students to visit the studio for a free 30-minute consultation. You'll get to see the gear, meet the team, and ask all your questions in person.
Do you accept collaboration or guest bookings?
Yes! We welcome independent artists and producers who want to use our studio on an hourly or daily basis. Contact us with your requirements and we'll share our availability and rates.
How do I get to the studio?
We're located in Lajpat Nagar, New Delhi — a 5-minute walk from Lajpat Nagar Metro Station (Pink Line). Parking is available on the premises for 2-wheelers. Click "Get Directions" on the map above for a full route.
